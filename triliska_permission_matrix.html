AD
Admin (Programme Admin)
Manages the entire programme. Creates all user types. Owns system configuration and has unrestricted access to all companies, reports, and audit logs.
Create all users All companies System config All reports Audit logs Record tax payments
PS
Programme Supervisor
Manages a team of programme staff. Creates staff accounts, assigns them to companies, reviews their caseloads, and views KPI reports for both staff and the companies under them.
Create programme staff Assign staff to companies Companies (staff's only) Staff KPI reports Sales / POS
PF
Programme Staff (NSP / Field Officer)
Field worker who registers MSMEs, loads products and inventory on the POS, and helps with onboarding. Can record sales if permitted.
Assigned companies only Register MSMEs Load products & inventory Record sales on POS (if permitted) View reports
BO
Business Owner
Owner of the MSME. Full control over their business on the platform. The only role that can initiate tax payments. Creates and manages business staff with configurable permissions.
Full POS access Tax payments (exclusive) Create business staff Assign staff permissions All business reports
PU
Business Power User
Like an accountant or manager — trusted employee of the owner with elevated access. Permissions are explicitly granted by the Business Owner; they can do almost everything including initiate tax payments if permitted.
Permissions set by owner POS (if granted) Reports (if granted) Tax payments Create other users
BS
Business Staff
Cashier or employee working for an MSME. Access limited to what the Business Owner explicitly allows — typically POS sales. Cannot access reports, products, or any financial settings by default.
POS sales (if granted) Stock movements (if granted) Products / inventory setup Reports Tax anything
GR
GRA Staff / Officer
Government officer with read-only access. Views and downloads tax compliance reports, submission status, and payment histories across all companies. Cannot modify any data.
View all submissions View all payments Download reports Modify any record POS / business ops
Items to remove from the backend remove
✕
Payments nav item — hide from all non-owner views
The Payments section (with "Pay Now" button) should only be visible to Business Owners. Remove it from Admin, Programme Staff, Supervisor, and GRA views entirely. Admin can see payment history for oversight but cannot initiate a payment.
✕
Submissions section — red-X it until requirements are confirmed
As flagged in the Q&A doc, the current Submissions screen has unclear requirements and mixes Odoo Import and CSV sources. The flow needs to be specified: data should feed the Triliska database directly from the POS/backend without requiring a separate re-submission to GRA. Disable this nav item until the Aliska middleware endpoint contract is defined.
✕
SGD currency — replace with GHS throughout
All currency displays currently show SGD (Singapore Dollar). All monetary values must be in GHS (Ghana Cedis). This is a critical correctness issue — the system is for Ghana.
✕
"Admin" label in top-right — rename to Programme Admin
The logged-in user label shows "admin". It should reflect the role display name: "Programme Admin" (or the user's actual name with role badge). This avoids confusion with business-level admin concepts.
Items to add to the backend dashboard add
+
Programme Staff section — staff list, assignments, and activity
Admin and Programme Supervisors need a dedicated section to: view all programme staff, see which companies each staff member is assigned to, track onboarding progress per staff, and review recent activity. Currently there is no staff management screen beyond "Account & Staff".
+
Onboarding progress tracker on dashboard
Admin and Supervisors should see a KPI card or progress bar showing: total MSMEs registered, number with products loaded, number active on POS, number with at least one sale recorded. This tracks the pipeline from registration to go-live.
+
Zone / district filter on Companies and Dashboard
The Companies list should filter by district and zone so programme supervisors can instantly scope to their area. The dashboard KPI cards should also scope to the logged-in user's zone automatically.
+
Tax compliance status column on Companies list
Add a "Compliance status" column to the company list showing: Not started / Products loaded / Sales active / Submission pending / Up to date. This gives programme staff and supervisors an at-a-glance operational status for every MSME without drilling into each record.
+
Role-scoped dashboard — different views per role
The current dashboard is a single view. It needs to be role-aware: Admin sees programme-wide KPIs. Supervisor sees their staff's companies. Business Owner sees their own business metrics (revenue, tax due, stock alerts). GRA Staff sees compliance and submission summary. Each role should land on a dashboard relevant to them.
+
Business Owner dashboard — business metrics not tax-only
When a Business Owner logs in, their dashboard should show: total sales today/week/month, stock alerts (low stock items), pending tax amount, and recent transactions. Currently the dashboard shows only tax payable — not useful for daily business operations.
+
GRA Staff dashboard — compliance reporting view
GRA Officers need a dedicated read-only view: number of registered MSMEs, number with pending tax submissions, total tax collected to date, and ability to download reports by period, district, and sector. No business operations data visible.
Items that need clarification before building clarify
?
Submission flow — who triggers it and how?
The Q&A doc says "data should go directly into a database, no need to submit again to GRA." This needs to be clarified: does the backend auto-generate submissions on a schedule (e.g. monthly), or does the Business Owner manually trigger each submission from the Business Portal? The current demo shows a manual "Submit Company Info" button — confirm if this is correct or should be automated.
?
POS sync target — Odoo or custom backend?
This is the unresolved architecture decision from the alignment analysis. The backend currently has "Odoo Import" as a submission source type, suggesting Odoo is in the loop. This must be decided before the Submissions section can be properly designed.
